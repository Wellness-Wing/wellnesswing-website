Wellness Wing

A multidisciplinary NGO & Wellness Initiative working in mental health, physical health, animal welfare, research and social welfare — with a primary focus on accessible mental healthcare.

Welcome to Wellness Wing

Wellness Wing is a multidisciplinary NGO and wellness initiative based in Mirzapur, Uttar Pradesh. Our primary focus is mental health, while we also work in physical health, animal welfare, research, and social welfare.


We believe that mental health is the foundation of a healthy individual, family, and society. Through awareness programs, professional services, and community outreach, we aim to make care accessible and stigma-free.


Under Wellness Wing, we also operate the Wellness Wing Multispeciality Clinic & Research Center, a professional, fee-based clinic that supports sustainable, ethical, and evidence-based healthcare while strengthening the social mission of the NGO.

Our Services

Mental Health Services

Depression, anxiety, stress, addiction, trauma, sleep and sexual health concerns.

Psychological Assessment

Standardized psychometric testing, IQ and personality assessment.

Psychotherapy & Counseling

CBT, supportive therapy and evidence-based psychological interventions.

Physical Health & Wellness

Mind–body wellness, lifestyle management and preventive care.

NGO Community Programs

Mental health camps, awareness drives, women empowerment and youth development.

Research & Training

Research-oriented clinical work, training and academic collaboration.
