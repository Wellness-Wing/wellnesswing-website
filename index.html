Future‑Ready Mental & Physical Healthcare

Wellness Wing is a modern NGO, Multispeciality Clinic & Research Center led by Dr. Amresh Kumar Yadav, dedicated to ethical, confidential and technology‑driven healthcare in Uttar Pradesh.

About Wellness Wing

Located in Krishnapuram Colony, Pakka Pokhara, Ramaipatti, Mirzapur (UP), Wellness Wing integrates mental health, physical health, rehabilitation, counselling, and research services under one futuristic ecosystem.

Our Services

Mental Health Services

Depression, anxiety, stress, addiction, trauma, sleep and sexual health issues.

Physical Health Support

Mind‑body wellness, lifestyle management and rehabilitation care.

Psychological & Psychometric Testing

Standardized and ethical assessments.

IQ & Personality Testing

Intelligence, aptitude and personality profiling for education and career planning.

Psychotherapy

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy, supportive therapy and evidence‑based interventions.

Clinical & Career Counselling

Professional guidance for mental health, academics and career decisions.

Rehabilitation Services

Psychiatric, neuro and disability rehabilitation with social reintegration.

NGO Community Programs

Mental health camps, awareness drives, women empowerment and youth development.
