Future-Ready Mental & Physical Healthcare

Wellness Wing is a multidisciplinary NGO & Multispeciality Clinic working primarily in mental health, along with physical health, research, animal welfare and social wellbeing.

About Wellness Wing

Located in Krishnapuram Colony, Pakka Pokhara, Ramaipatti, Mirzapur (UP), Wellness Wing integrates mental health, physical health, counselling, rehabilitation and research services under one ecosystem.

Our Services

Mental Health Services

Depression, anxiety, stress, trauma, addiction and sexual health issues.

Physical Health Support

Mind-body wellness, lifestyle management and rehabilitation.

Psychological Testing

Standardized psychometric, IQ and personality assessments.

Psychotherapy & Counselling

CBT and evidence-based therapeutic interventions.

NGO Community Programs

Mental health awareness, camps and social welfare activities.
