Wellness Wing

Wellness Wing is a multidisciplinary NGO, Multispeciality Clinic & Research Center working in mental health, physical health, animal welfare, research and social welfare, with a primary focus on accessible and ethical mental healthcare.

Welcome To Wellness Wing

Wellness Wing is a registered NGO and healthcare initiative based in Mirzapur, Uttar Pradesh. Under the NGO, A multidisciplinary NGO & wellness initiative dedicated to building a healthier, more compassionate society. Wellness Wing works primarily in the field of mental health, while also addressing physical health, animal welfare, research, and social welfare. Our goal is to make care, awareness, and support accessible to individuals and communities that are often left behind. Mental health is our core focus, because we believe emotional wellbeing is the foundation of a healthy individual, family, and society.

Our work spans community mental health, research, rehabilitation, counselling, women empowerment, youth development and social welfare programs.

What We Do

Provide accessible and affordable mental health support Promote physical and psychological wellbeing through awareness programs Work for animal welfare and humane care Encourage research‑based interventions and evidence‑informed practice Support social welfare initiatives for vulnerable communities

Why Wellness Wing

In many parts of India, mental and holistic healthcare remain either unavailable, unaffordable, or misunderstood. Wellness Wing was created to bridge this gap by combining professional expertise, community engagement, and ethical social work.

About Wellness Wing

Who We Are

Wellness Wing is a registered/non‑profit oriented NGO and wellness initiative based in Mirzapur, Uttar Pradesh. We function with a multidisciplinary approach, integrating mental health services, community welfare, and social responsibility under one platform. Our work is guided by the belief that true wellness goes beyond the absence of illnessit includes mental balance, physical health, social dignity, and compassion toward all living beings.

About Wellness Wing

Our Vision

To create a society where mental health is understood, accepted, and supported, To create a society where mental health is understood, accepted, and supported, and where holistic wellbeing and social responsibility are accessible to everyone, regardless of background or economic status.

About Wellness Wing

Our Mission

To promote mental health awareness and early intervention at the community level To provide ethical, affordable, and evidence-based care To integrate physical health, animal welfare, and social support into a holistic model of wellness To encourage research, education, and innovation in mental and community health

About Wellness Wing

Our Objective

To spread mental health awareness, reduce stigma, and promote emotional wellbeing in society To provide accessible psychological support and counseling services, especially for underserved populations To organize health awareness, preventive care, and wellness programs for holistic physical and mental health To promote and support animal welfare and humane treatment of animals To encourage and undertake research, training, and knowledge development related to mental health, wellness, and social issues To work for social welfare, education, and empowerment of vulnerable and marginalized communities To collaborate with government bodies, NGOs, institutions, and professionals for sustainable social impact To function with transparency, ethics, and social responsibility in all activities
