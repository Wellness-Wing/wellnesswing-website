Holistic Health with Purpose

Wellness Wing is a multidisciplinary NGO with an integrated Multispeciality Clinic & Research Center, working primarily in mental health while addressing physical health, research, animal welfare and social wellbeing through ethical, professional and sustainable care.

About Wellness Wing

Wellness Wing is a community-focused NGO based in Mirzapur, Uttar Pradesh. We believe that true wellness includes mental balance, physical health, social dignity and compassionate care.

Our work combines professional healthcare services with social responsibility, ensuring accessibility, ethics and long-term impact.

Our Objectives

Promote mental health awareness and reduce stigma in society

Provide ethical, accessible psychological and wellness support

Support physical health, preventive care and rehabilitation

Encourage research and evidence-based practice

Promote animal welfare and humane care

Work for social welfare and community empowerment

Multispeciality Clinic & Research Center

Under Wellness Wing, the Multispeciality Clinic & Research Center provides fee-based professional consultation in mental and physical health. This sustainable model supports high-quality care while strengthening NGO initiatives.

The clinic is led by a qualified Director and supported by trained professionals, following strict ethical and confidentiality standards.

Clinical & NGO Services

Mental Health Care

Counselling, psychotherapy and assessments

Physical Health Support

Wellness and lifestyle management

Psychological Testing

IQ, personality and psychometric assessments

Community Programs

Awareness camps and NGO initiatives
